## How It Works

The platform layers AI execution across the full lifecycle — product inception through governance — while preserving Nelnet's six-stage NAM structure, entry/exit gates, and Scheduled Releases cadence.

Three architectural properties keep it safe at scale:

- **Multi-reviewer validation** — Peer-reviewer agents challenge every AI output before human review
- **Audit evidence as byproduct** — Decision logs, traceability matrices, quality reports emitted automatically
- **Profile-driven tailoring** — NIST controls for Federal, consumer-financial controls for Commercial, isolated controls for restricted environments
