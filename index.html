Our Core Approach

AI generates the work product at every stage. Peer-reviewer agents validate before human eyes see it. Named roles own every gate. Governance evidence emits as a byproduct of delivery — not assembled after the fact.

The platform overlays the SDLC Nelnet already runs — preserving your six-stage NAM structure, entry/exit gates, and Scheduled Releases cadence. Three properties keep it safe at scale:

  • Multi-reviewer validation — Peer-reviewer agents challenge every AI output before human review
  • Audit evidence as byproduct — Decision logs, traceability matrices, quality reports emitted automatically
  • Profile-driven tailoring — NIST for Federal, consumer-financial for Commercial, isolated controls for restricted environments
  • Adoption-led transformation — Embedded coaching, role-based enablement, and champion networks ensure teams don't just understand the lifecycle — they operate differently within it, sustained by a COE that drives continuous improvement

The Compounding Advantage

As Nelnet operates within the lifecycle, organizational knowledge accumulates. Each initiative strengthens the next:

  • Requirements cycle time — reduced from weeks to less than a day
  • Test planning — reduced from days to less than a day
  • Evidence preparation — eliminated (emitted automatically)
  • Refactoring time — 60% reduction across legacy codebases
  • Adoption signal — teams apply the lifecycle to new initiatives without prompting
Representative Outputs

Real, version-controlled work products emitted at every stage — not assembled for review:

Artifact What It Proves
Decision Log Cumulative decisions with category traces & downstream impact
Requirements Decomposition Business Use Case → Epic → Story → Dev Package, AI-generated
Document History A recorded history of all changes made to the input data at every stage.
Quality Report PASS/WARN/FAIL verdicts per gate with gap analysis
Peer-Review Records Multi-agent validation evidence for every AI output
Contradiction Reconciliation Conflicts between new sources and finalized decisions are flagged and routed for review, keeping the original content intact.
Provenance & Source Traceability Each document includes a self-updating history of changes and a table linking every section to its original source, eliminating the need to manually build an audit trail.
Decomposition Plan Each task is split into structured development packages, checked against the real codebase for accuracy, and approved by an engineering lead before any code is generated.
Quality Report Test results are automatically generated and linked to specific requirements, giving the release approver clear, traceable evidence to review rather than a simple pass or fail.
